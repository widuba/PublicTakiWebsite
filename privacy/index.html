Taki AI Assistant

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This policy explains what information Taki AI Assistant (“Taki,” “we,” “us”) collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Taki is operated by Christopher Nelson Barlow. Questions? Email Taki AI Support.

The short version: Taki keeps as much as possible on your device. We only send what's needed to answer a request, we never sell your data, we don't use it for advertising, and we don't track you across other apps or websites.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following only to make the app work and personalize your experience. It's linked to your account or device identifier so we can meter usage and provide features.

DataWhy we collect it
Your nameTo personalize how the assistant addresses and responds to you.
Email addressOnly if you choose Sign in with Apple, to create and restore your account.
Precise locationOnly when a feature needs it (travel times, weather, “near me”). Sent per request; not continuously tracked.
Photos you attachWhen you ask about a photo, that image is sent to be analyzed.
Voice recordingsIn voice mode, your recording is sent to be transcribed so the assistant can respond.
Requests & profile textThe questions and context you send are processed to generate an answer or perform an action.
Feedback you submitStored so we can review and improve the app.
Account / device IDTo authenticate you, meter credits, prevent fraud, and keep your subscription in sync.
Purchase recordsTo manage your subscription and grant the credits you paid for.
Network (IP) addressLogged for security, fraud prevention, and enforcing our Terms of Service.

2. Information that stays on your device

These are accessed on your device to perform actions you ask for and are not uploaded to our servers:

  • Your contacts and address book
  • Your calendar and reminders
  • Health and fitness data
  • Your music and photo library
  • Home & device automations

3. How we use your information

We use the information above only to:

  • Provide the app's features (answers, actions, voice, live activities, subscriptions).
  • Personalize responses to you.
  • Authenticate you, prevent fraud, and keep the service reliable and secure.
  • Detect abuse and enforce our Terms — including flagging and, where necessary, reviewing content that appears illegal or seriously harmful, and suspending or banning accounts, devices, or IP addresses.

We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell or share it with data brokers, and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

4. Service providers

To operate Taki, limited data is processed by trusted providers acting on our behalf:

  • Google — processes your requests (and attached photos/searches) to generate answers.
  • ElevenLabs — transcribes and synthesizes speech for voice mode.
  • Apple — handles Sign in with Apple and all subscription payments (we never receive your card details).
  • Hosting & database providers — run our servers and securely store account, credit, and feedback data.

These providers may process your data only to perform their service for us, subject to their own privacy terms.

5. Data retention

Most requests are processed in real time and aren't retained after your answer is delivered. We do store account and credit information, feedback, and any alerts you create for as long as your account is active. You can ask us to delete this at any time.

Safety exception: to keep Taki safe and lawful, our automated safety systems may flag and retain messages that appear to involve illegal or seriously harmful activity, together with related device and network (IP) information. Such retained messages are reviewed by us only if your account is flagged for repeated violations, and may be used to enforce our Terms of Service (including suspending or permanently banning accounts, devices, and IP addresses).

6. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), and account data is stored on secured infrastructure. No method is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Children

Taki is not directed to children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

8. Your choices & rights

  • Revoke Taki's access to location, photos, microphone, contacts, calendar, and health data anytime in iOS Settings.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions.
  • Request access to, or deletion of, your account data by emailing us.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA, including to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise them, contact us.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact

Questions or requests? Email Taki AI Support.
